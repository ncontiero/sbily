Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sbily?

Sbily is an open-source link shortener that allows you to create shorter and easier-to-share links. With it, you can manage all your links in one place.

How does a temporary link work?

Temporary links automatically expire after the period you define. This is useful for sharing content that should only be available for a limited time.

What happens if I reach my link limit?

On the free plan, you will need to delete existing links to create new ones when you reach the limit. On the Premium plan, you can purchase additional slots to increase your capacity.

How can I contribute to the project?

As an open-source project, you can contribute code, report bugs, or suggest improvements through our repository on GitHub.
